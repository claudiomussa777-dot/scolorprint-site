# Smart Color Print — visual assets v1

These project-bound mockups were produced with the built-in image-generation tool. They are commercial catalog visuals, not photographs of completed client work. Real production photographs remain in `assets/trabalhos/`.

## Shared prompt direction

- Use case: `product-mockup`
- Premium international commercial product photography.
- Warm off-white paper studio, cobalt-blue sculptural backdrop, ink-black geometry, and tiny magenta/cyan/acid-yellow registration accents.
- Softbox lighting, realistic material texture, controlled shadows, responsive-crop margins.
- No words, logos, watermarks, fake company names, clutter, or rainbow palette.

## Asset prompts

- `hero-studio-v1.webp`: coordinated collection with a white T-shirt, roll-up, business cards, mug, and premium paper gift bag.
- `tshirt-studio-v1.webp`: cropped torso wearing a white cotton T-shirt with abstract geometric chest print.
- `rollup-studio-v1.webp`: complete freestanding roll-up with accurate brushed-aluminum mechanism and abstract artwork.
- `cards-studio-v1.webp`: thick uncoated business-card stack with two loose cards, macro three-quarter view.
- `vinyl-studio-v1.webp`: modern boutique window with professionally applied geometric printed and frosted vinyl.
- `gifts-studio-v1.webp`: coordinated corporate set with one mug, two metal pens, notebook, and matte gift box.

The Hero and catalog use these mockups. The portfolio intentionally continues to use actual Smart Color Print production photographs.
